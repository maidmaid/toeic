# Complements

3 types of subject complements:

1. `Adjective` complement:
   - She is `beautiful`.
   - She appeared `happy`.
   - The food tates `delicious`.
2. `Adverb` complement:
   - I am `at the beach`.
   - She drives `carefully`.
   - The children are playing `upstairs`.
3. `Noun` complement:
   - John is `a teacher`.
   - He is a great `athlete`.
